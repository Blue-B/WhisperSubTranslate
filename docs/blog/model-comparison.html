Our Recommendations

For most users: Start with small or turbo. They offer the best balance of speed and accuracy for everyday use.

By Use Case

  • YouTube/Podcasts: small or turbo
  • Movies/TV Shows: medium or large-v3
  • Quick Drafts: tiny or base
  • Professional/Broadcast: large-v3
  • Multiple Languages: medium or large-v3

By Hardware

  • 4GB VRAM or less: tiny, base, small
  • 6GB VRAM: small, medium, turbo
  • 8GB+ VRAM: Any model including large-v3
  • CPU only: tiny or base recommended
